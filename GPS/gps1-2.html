What is pair programming

Pair programming is incorporating the use of two programmers and two sets of eyes into finding one solution. A lot of agile teams like to use pair programming.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

This gets you used to working together. It also makes sure both people's opinions are incorporated into the problem. Double checks for syntactical and logical errors. Keeps ideas flowing and able to bounce off one another.

Compare Driver and Navigator

Driver is a hands on role, and navigator decides what to focus on. This allows you to take a more specific mindset.
